GENERAL INFORMATION

For your comfort, the Health Service Unit is a "walk in" clinic. Through the referral channel, clients are seen by appointment for the doctor or other specialists. The clinic offers Primary Health Care services such as health education, health and wellbeing, health protection, health promotion, and counseling. All clients are encouraged to visit the clinic in the morning if they are not feeling well in order to be sent to specialists if necessary.

Vision


The objective of Health Services includes offering assessments and aiding students, employees, and visitors with health-related concerns through a holistic Primary Health care approach to health care management. Health Services also offers health awareness and illness prevention programming to the University community.
